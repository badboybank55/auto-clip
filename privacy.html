Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy explains how เงินงอก (NgernNgork), operated by Apisit Sangthong ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information in connection with our automated video publishing pipeline and our use of the TikTok Content Posting API and other platform APIs.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do NOT Collect from Viewers

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from video viewers on TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, or Instagram. We do not operate a website that requires viewer registration or login.

1.2 Information Collected Through Platform APIs

When using the TikTok Content Posting API and other social media APIs, we access only the following data strictly necessary for automated video publishing:

  • TikTok: Channel owner's TikTok account identifier (open_id) and publishing permissions (video.publish scope) — used solely to upload videos to our own channel.
  • YouTube: Channel owner's Google account credentials via OAuth 2.0 — used solely to upload videos and thumbnails to our own YouTube channel.
  • Facebook: Page Access Token for our own Facebook Page — used solely to publish video content to our own page.
  • Instagram: Instagram Business Account credentials — used solely to publish Reels to our own Instagram account.

All API credentials are stored locally on the operator's private machine and are never shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Information

API access tokens and credentials are used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram APIs on behalf of the channel owner
  • Upload and publish video content to our own social media channels
  • Retrieve channel configuration data (e.g., video duration limits, privacy settings)

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose beyond automated content publishing.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

Our application uses the TikTok Content Posting API with the video.publish scope. In compliance with TikTok's Platform Terms:

  • We access only the minimum data necessary for video publishing
  • We do not store, cache, or transfer TikTok user data to any third party
  • Access tokens are stored securely on the local machine of the channel operator only
  • We do not access any data beyond what is granted by the video.publish scope
  • The application acts solely on behalf of the channel owner — no third-party users are involved

4. Data Storage and Security

API credentials and tokens are stored in a local .env file on the channel operator's private Mac computer. This file is:

  • Excluded from version control (listed in .gitignore)
  • Never transmitted to any third-party server
  • Protected by macOS system-level access controls

Generated videos are temporarily uploaded to Cloudinary for Instagram delivery and are immediately and automatically deleted upon successful publishing.

5. Third-Party Services

Our pipeline integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
